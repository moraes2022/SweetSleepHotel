    Discover the Sweet Sleep Hotel

    Nestled in a private area of the shimmering lagoon, THE Sweet Sleep is a first-of-its-kind luxury residence featuring three bedrooms and integrated living, dining, and entertaining spaces. The experience is complete with 24-hour private butler service, a private chef, and tailored experiences.

    Summer in the Maldives Islands

    Summer Island Maldives is a truly beautiful, new 'barefoot' resort owned and managed by people from the Maldives. Truly the perfect Maldivian Barefoot Holiday where Summer Never Ends. A holiday at Summer is really a journey of discovery more than a holiday. Take a seaplane to a fairytale lagoon and walk barefoot on fine sand the colour of icing sugar and feel it tickle your soles.
